Animal Agriculture is Factory Farming

In the United States, 99% of farmed animals spend their lives on factory farms.

About this project

This project was created for IS 180 (History and Practice of Information Visualization) at UCLA in Fall 2021. This website contains four sections, each with a different visualization of information related to animal agriculture as it is currently practiced in California, or in the United States more generally when state-specific data was not available. Cows, chickens, and pigs are the animals focused on here, as they are the most popular sources of animal products in the United States.

When possible, all information used to produce these visualizations was taken from industry-affiliated sources (such as agricultural journals and government regulatory agencies) rather than from activist sources. Citations for data and assets used in each visualization can be found on individual pages.
