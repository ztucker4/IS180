Chicken Space

In California, chicken farmers are legally required to allocate 1 square foot per chicken, and cages may not be used. What would this look like if similar requirements were in place for humans?

By comparing the ratio of the average chicken wingspan to the area required per chicken and scaling this up to a human armspan value, I found that a football field would accommodate 3,413 humans.
